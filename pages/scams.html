Scams and frauds are deceptive practices intended to steal money, personal information, or financial data.

What is a Financial Scam?

A financial scam is an illegal activity where fraudsters trick individuals into giving money or sensitive information.

Common Types of Scams

  • Online frauds
  • Phishing emails and messages
  • Investment scams
  • Loan frauds
  • UPI and OTP scams

Warning Signs of Scams

  • Promises of unrealistic returns
  • Urgent requests for payment
  • Requests for OTP, PIN, or passwords
  • Unknown links or calls

How to Protect Yourself

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Verify sources before transactions
  • Use secure payment platforms
  • Report suspicious activities

Conclusion

Awareness is the best defense against financial scams. Staying informed helps protect money and personal information.
