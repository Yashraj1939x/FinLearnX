What is a Loan?

A loan is money borrowed from a lender with an agreement to repay the amount along with interest over a fixed period.

  • Education Loan
  • Home Loan
  • Vehicle Loan
  • Business Loan

What is Debt?

Debt refers to the total amount of money owed by an individual or organization to lenders.
